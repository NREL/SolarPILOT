Markets

Page Overview


The market page is used to determine how power produced throughout the year is valued. Define the time-of-use dispatch schedule and weighting factors for power produced during various hours and months of the year.


Weekday / Weekend Schedule


Payment Allocation Factors




Weekday / Weekend Schedule

- Top of page -


The weekday and weekend schedule controls allow you to specify how each hour of the day (by month) is assigned to time-of-use (TOU) categories. The weekday schedule is applied for all days of the week Monday-Friday, while the weekend schedule is applied for all days of the week Saturday and Sunday. 


The schedule is constructed with each month of the year along the vertical axis and each hour of the day on the horizontal axis. 



The hourly values indicate the starting point of the hour that they represent. 


Manipulating table entries

You can modify the values in the schedule table as follows:

  1. Using your mouse, click and drag to select the set of values you'd like to modify.
  2. With the region selected, press the number key for the period you'd like to assign to the selected values. 


Payment Allocation Factors

- Top of page -


The payment allocation factors provide weighting factors for the power produced during a particular time of the year. If enabled, the factors multiply the power produced by the value that you specify. In this way, the power produced by the system is valued according to the market pricing. 


The values that you specify in each of the Payment Allocation Periods correspond to the period numbers that you specify in the Weekday/Weekend schedules. You may specify up to 9 distinct periods. 


Enable Payment Weighting Factors

Specify whether the factor values will be applied. If this option is disabled, all weighting factors are assumed to be equal.


Payment Allocation - Period 1-9

The weighting factor corresponding to the TOU period indicated on the Weekday/Weekend schedule.
