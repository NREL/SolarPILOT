Sales Tax Rate

[%] Sales tax rate that is applied to direct plant costs.

Total Installed Costs

- Top of page -


The total installed cost represents the expected cost incurred to construct the plant. This cost omits financing, construction loan, and operation & maintenance costs.


Total Installed Cost

[$] Total installed cost is the sum of the total direct cost and the total indirect cost, as defined above.


Estimated Cost Per Capacity

[$] The total estimated cost per capacity is equal to the total installed cost divided by the Solar Field Design Power from the Layout Setup page divided by 1,000.
